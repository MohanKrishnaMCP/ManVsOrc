MAN VS ORC

Theme- 1 vs 1 game, 
if man hits the orc with bullet he gets 1 points, if orc touches man he dies and he respawn with score -5.

----Run ManvsOrc.py to play the game--------

####---CONTROLS---####

BULLETS	- Space bar
LEFT	- Left arrow
RIGHT	- Right arrow
JUMP	- Jump arrow
